The tool for developing people

Transparent marketplace for education - online and offline. Digitization of further education process in HR. Evaluation systems and individual matching through AI.

The problems we face

Changing labour market

That digitization is about to disrupt labour market is no secret. 55% of employees have to re-educate until 2022.

Scattered marketplace

There are dozens of online course platforms not to mention all the places to inform about offline trainings. A comparison is hard to nearly impossible.

Insufficient valuation of trainings

The current valuation is mainly based on subjective ratings and not on the gain of competence of attendees.

How we're solving them

1

Build a web application that helps HR departments managing personnel development to reduce costs and enhance results.

2

Extend the application by a transparent marketplace for job trainings offered by the training providers directly.

3

Open the market place to private users. Individuals can manage their own education path easily using the private section.

4

Expand the marketplace by online courses. Providers can add their trainings offered and hosted on course platforms.

5

Creation of an AI for matchmaking between learners and trainings, individuals and education paths, jobs and employees.

6

Allow the evaluation of trainings and peoples competence by using AI. Create a new standard for the measurement of skill.
